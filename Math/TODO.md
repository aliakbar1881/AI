## Directory: Mathematics
## Topics that must be covered
- **Linear Algebra**: Vectors, Matrices, Tensor operations, Eigenvalues, Eigenvectors, SVD
- **Calculus**: Derivatives, Partial derivatives, Gradients, Chain rule (backpropagation core), Integrals
- **Probability**: Bayes' Theorem, Conditional probability, Distributions (Gaussian, Bernoulli, Poisson), MLE, MAP
- **Statistics**: Mean, Median, Mode, Variance, Standard deviation, Hypothesis testing, P-values, Confidence intervals
- **Optimization**: Convex functions, Global vs. Local minima, Gradient Descent intuition
- **Information Theory**: Entropy, Cross-Entropy, KL Divergence
